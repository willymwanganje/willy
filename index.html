OVERVIEW OF THE 7th CONVOCATION OF THE EASTERN AFRICA STATISTICAL TRAINING CENTRE(EASTC)


The 7th convocation of the eastern africa statistical training centre of the year 2024 continues te tradition within academic institutions of gathering together academicians ,students,alumn,benefactors, and industry professionals as stakeholders of the university community.It is the time to excghange views on topical issues that touch teaching, research and professional development.


The year's convocation has selected the topic "THE ROLE OF OFFICIAL STATISTICS AND BIG DATA IN THE ECONOMIC TRANSFORMATION AND SUSTAINABLE DEVELOPMENT OF TANZANIA" as the standing point to reflect on theme of the year's 18 of november african statistics day.The theme states that "MODERNIZING DATA ECOSYSTEMS ACCERERATE THE IMPLEMENTATION OF AFRICAN CONTINENTAL FREE TRADE AREA (AFCFTA): THE ROLE OF OFFICIAL STATISTICS AND BIG DATA IN THE ECONOMIC TRANSFORMATION AND SUSTAINABLEDEVELOPMENT OF AFRICA



African statistics day is celebrated each year on 18th of november.the commemoration aims among others, to increase public awareness about the importnt role which statististics play in all aspects of social and economic development.this year's convocation aims to understand the african continental free trade araa(AFCFTA) and the potential role of statistics and big data in discovering of markets and serving customers across africa "
